**TEALS Program** Curriculum Map | Introduction | Change Log # Do Now 0.04 I am How would you fill in the following: I am ______? This could be answered in many ways: 1. What you enjoy doing: I am a musician. 2. Physically: I am tall. 3. Personal characteristic: I am funny. 4. Career aspirations: I am an engineer. 5. Self reflective: I am a deep thinker. 6. As an athlete: I am a runner. And this is just the beginning. After taking few minutes to think who you are, write down a few. We will be using who you are to write a Snap self expression program.
